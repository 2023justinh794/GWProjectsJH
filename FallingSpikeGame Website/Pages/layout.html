What does the layout consist of?

As you can see by looking at the picture below, there are 2 diffferent layouts. One for the "Home" screen and the other for the "game" screen when you actually play the game. On the home screen, there are 2 buttons. Clicking the top one brings you to the game screen where you can play the game. Clicking the bottom one opens a new window into a site called "coolmathgames". In the game screen, the player is the white square on the platform. There is a textbox keeping track of your score and when you lose the game, the "game over" sprites pop up at the top with 2 buttons. One to play again and the other to bring you back to the home screen.
